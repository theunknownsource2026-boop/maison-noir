The Architect Coat

€2,400

A structured overcoat built on the tension between rigid form and fluid movement. Cut from a single bolt of Italian double-faced wool, each coat is hand-finished over 40 hours in our Paris atelier. The oversized silhouette falls to mid-calf, with raw-edge seams that will soften and fray uniquely with wear.

Size Size Guide
  • 100% Italian double-faced virgin wool
  • Silk lining (100% mulberry silk)
  • Horn buttons, hand-polished
  • Weight: 1,200g
  • Dry clean only
  • Store on wide-shouldered hanger
  • Avoid prolonged direct sunlight
  • Natural fibers will develop character over time
  • Complimentary worldwide shipping
  • Made to order: 2-3 weeks production
  • 14-day return policy (unworn, original packaging)
  • Each piece ships in a custom garment bag

Each Architect Coat is individually numbered and accompanied by a certificate of authenticity. The fabric is sourced from a single-estate mill in Biella, Italy, operational since 1897. Our atelier in the 3rd arrondissement of Paris handles every step from pattern cutting to final pressing.
